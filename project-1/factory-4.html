Types of Mixed Economy
Social Market Economy:
This subtype combines elements of both capitalism and socialism. It maintains a free market for goods and services while also providing social welfare programs and government regulation to address inequality and market failures.
State Capitalism:
In state capitalism, the government plays a significant role in the economy, owning or controlling key industries and resources. However, there is still room for private enterprise and market competition.
