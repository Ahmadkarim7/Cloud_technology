What is E-commerce?

To better understand in easy words E-commerce is a method in which you buy and sell goods through online.

How it works?

The idea is simple that is you open your online store. For example if you want to open a physical store in a place you will need to find out the market place first. The same thing happens with online store too. First you need to find out the bext market place for you. There are many market places but the well known places are the following

  • Amazon
  • Ebay
  • Walmart
  • Shopify

We will start this session from Amazon

There is a system called BSR(BEST Selling Rank), every product has its own BSR. BSR shows the demand of the product. If a product have BSR under 10k; that means this product have high demand. By all means the lower the BSR the best the product to choose for selling. All you need to find a product under this requirement. Once you find out the product. Now you have to find the supplier.

Where Can we find supplier

There are bunch of ways do find suppliers. But the best one is Alibaba where you can find a supplier in cheap prices.
