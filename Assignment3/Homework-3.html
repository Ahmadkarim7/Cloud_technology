Rolex was invented in London 1905 by Hans Wilsdorf and Alfred Davis later they moved their company from London to Switzerland. Rolex has a huge fan base around the world. Their prices are in hundred and thousands of dollars.

Michael Kors was founded by the American fashion designer Michael Kors in 1981. Their brand gained popularity for its luxurious accessible desgins. Their wathces are not expensive as compare to other brands, but their styles are very popular among the young generation.
Omega
Omega was established in 1848 in Switzerland, Omega has been making watches over a centuary. Omega watches are highly regarded for their durabilitty, accuracy, and elegant design. Most of their consumers are astronauts, athletes, and explorers.
